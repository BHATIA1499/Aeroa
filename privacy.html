Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026

Skuvvy ("we", "us", "our") provides AI-powered merchandising and trading analytics. This policy explains what personal and business data we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the rights you have over it. By using Skuvvy you agree to the practices described below.

1. Who we are

Skuvvy is the data controller for the personal data processed through our service. If you have any questions about this policy or your data, contact us at hello@aeroa.ai.

2. Data we collect

  • Account data — your name, email address, and authentication identifiers when you sign up (including via Google sign-in).
  • Trading & merchandising data — the sales, stock, product, and performance data you upload or connect for analysis.
  • Billing data — subscription plan and payment records. Card details are handled directly by our payment processor and are never stored on our servers.
  • Usage data — log data, device/browser information, and product interactions used to keep the service secure and improve it.

3. How we use your data

  • To provide merchandising recommendations, forecasts, and trading insights.
  • To create and manage your account and subscription.
  • To secure the service, prevent fraud, and meet legal obligations.
  • To communicate with you about your account and important service updates.

4. AI processing

To generate insights, relevant portions of your data may be processed by our third-party AI provider. We do not permit your data to be used to train third-party foundation models. Where available, we apply data-masking and private-processing controls, which you can manage in your Security Centre.

5. Service providers we share data with

  • Supabase — authentication and encrypted database hosting.
  • Stripe — payment processing and subscription billing.
  • Anthropic — AI model processing for generating insights.

These providers process data on our behalf under contractual and security obligations. We do not sell your personal data.

6. Data security

Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use role-based access controls, audit logging, and bank-grade encryption to protect your information. No system is perfectly secure, but we work continuously to safeguard your data.

7. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you close your account, we delete or anonymise your data within a reasonable period, except where we must retain it to meet legal or accounting obligations.

8. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, email hello@aeroa.ai.

9. International transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own. Where this happens, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at hello@aeroa.ai.
