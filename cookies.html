Cookie Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Skuvvy uses cookies and similar technologies when you use our website and application. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site work, remember your preferences, and keep you securely signed in.

2. How we use cookies

We keep our use of cookies to a minimum. We rely on cookies primarily to run the core service — not for advertising.

TypePurpose
Strictly necessaryKeep you signed in, secure your session, and protect against fraud (including CSRF protection). The service cannot function without these.
PreferencesRemember choices such as dismissed banners and settings (some stored in your browser's local storage rather than cookies).
AnalyticsHelp us understand how the product is used so we can improve it. These are used sparingly and never to identify you personally.

3. Third-party cookies

Some cookies may be set by trusted providers that power parts of our service, such as Stripe (secure payments) and our authentication provider. These are used to deliver the functionality you request.

4. Managing cookies

Most browsers let you view, manage, and delete cookies through their settings. Blocking strictly-necessary cookies may prevent you from signing in or using core features of Skuvvy.

5. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
