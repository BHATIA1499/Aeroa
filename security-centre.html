📜 Anthropic Data Policy
Model Training: Your data is never used to train AI models. This is enforced by Anthropic's commercial API agreement.
Data Retention (Anthropic): Anthropic retains API inputs/outputs for up to 30 days for trust & safety purposes only, then deletes them.
No Third-Party Sharing: Anthropic does not share your data with third parties.
Recommendation: Enable Private Processing Mode for the highest level of data confidentiality, especially for luxury and heritage brand clients.
